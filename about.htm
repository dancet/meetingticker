About

Meetings are a fact of life in the workplace. Unfortunately, most meetings lack planning and are executed poorly. Meeting ticker is a tool to help make meetings more effective and enjoyable.

Meeting Ticker can help you with the following problems:

  • Meetings with little or no point
  • Attendees rambling off topic
  • Blank stares
  • Losing hours of your day to pointless meetings
  • Meetings running longer than scheduled

Meeting Ticker has been developed in my spare time with a view to improve meetings in my workplace. If Meeting Ticker assists you in running a great meeting, I'd love to hear from you! I'm on Twitter @tomdance or you can email me tom.dance@gmail.com. I'm also keen to get bug reports or other constructive feedback on Meeting Ticker.

Meeting Ticker requires a JavaScript enabled browser. If you have JavaScript turned off, maybe you should consider selling your computer.
